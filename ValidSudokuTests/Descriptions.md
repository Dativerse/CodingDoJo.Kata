# Valid Sudoku

You are given a 9 x 9 Sudoku board. A Sudoku board is valid if the following rules are followed:

1. Each row must contain the digits 1-9 without duplicates.
2. Each column must contain the digits 1-9 without duplicates.
3. Each of the nine 3 x 3 sub-boxes of the grid must contain the digits 1-9 without duplicates.

Return `true` if the Sudoku board is valid, otherwise return `false`.

**Note:** A board does not need to be full or be solvable to be valid.

## Example 1

**Input:**
```
board =
[["1","2",".",".","3",".",".",".","."],
 ["4",".",".","5",".",".",".",".","."],
 [".","9","8",".",".",".",".",".","3"],
 ["5",".",".",".","6",".",".",".","4"],
 [".",".",".","8",".","3",".",".","5"],
 ["7",".",".",".","2",".",".",".","6"],
 [".",".",".",".",".",".","2",".","."],
 [".",".",".","4","1","9",".",".","8"],
 [".",".",".",".","8",".",".","7","9"]]
```

**Visual:**
```
┌───────┬───────┬───────┐
│ 1 2 . │ . 3 . │ . . . │
│ 4 . . │ 5 . . │ . . . │
│ . 9 8 │ . . . │ . . 3 │
├───────┼───────┼───────┤
│ 5 . . │ . 6 . │ . . 4 │
│ . . . │ 8 . 3 │ . . 5 │
│ 7 . . │ . 2 . │ . . 6 │
├───────┼───────┼───────┤
│ . . . │ . . . │ 2 . . │
│ . . . │ 4 1 9 │ . . 8 │
│ . . . │ . 8 . │ . 7 9 │
└───────┴───────┴───────┘
```

**Output:** `true` — No duplicates in any row, column, or 3x3 box.

## Example 2

**Input:**
```
board =
[["1","2",".",".","3",".",".",".","."],
 ["4",".",".","5",".",".",".",".","."],
 [".","9","1",".",".",".",".",".","3"],
 ["5",".",".",".","6",".",".",".","4"],
 [".",".",".","8",".","3",".",".","5"],
 ["7",".",".",".","2",".",".",".","6"],
 [".",".",".",".",".",".","2",".","."],
 [".",".",".","4","1","9",".",".","8"],
 [".",".",".",".","8",".",".","7","9"]]
```

**Visual:**
```
┌───────┬───────┬───────┐
│ 1 2 . │ . 3 . │ . . . │
│ 4 . . │ 5 . . │ . . . │
│ . 9 1 │ . . . │ . . 3 │  ← 1 here
├───────┼───────┼───────┤
│ 5 . . │ . 6 . │ . . 4 │
│ . . . │ 8 . 3 │ . . 5 │
│ 7 . . │ . 2 . │ . . 6 │
├───────┼───────┼───────┤
│ . . . │ . . . │ 2 . . │
│ . . . │ 4 1 9 │ . . 8 │
│ . . . │ . 8 . │ . 7 9 │
└───────┴───────┴───────┘
```

**Output:** `false`

**Explanation:** The top-left 3x3 box contains two `1`s (at positions [0,0] and [2,2]).

## Constraints

- `board.length == 9`
- `board[i].length == 9`
- `board[i][j]` is a digit 1-9 or `'.'`.
